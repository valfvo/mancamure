Purpose

Collect at least 25 seeds

How to play

You can play with the 6 pits in front of you.
When you click on a pit, you'll dispatch one by one all seeds
you have inside.
Example : 6 seeds in the pit, the next 6 pits (counter
clockwise) take a seed. The pit played have not seed left.
